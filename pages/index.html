  Exploring Our Project Performance Indices

Our repository uses 27 carefully selected indices to provide a comprehensive view of project performance. These are divided into qualitative and quantitative metrics:

    Qualitative Indices:

  • Project Relevance
  • Stakeholder Satisfaction
  • Quality of Project Planning
  • Quality of Execution
  • Risk Management Effectiveness
  • Communication Effectiveness
  • Teamwork Quality
  • Quality of Deliverables
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Leadership Quality
  • Sustainability
  • Health and Safety Compliance
These indices capture subjective aspects of project performance, providing insights into areas that are crucial for success but not easily quantified.

    Quantitative Indices:

  • Budget Utilization
  • Schedule Performance Index (SPI)
  • Cost Performance Index (CPI)
  • Project Efficiency
  • Return on Investment (ROI)
  • Benefit-Cost Ratio (BCR)
  • Net Present Value (NPV)
  • Internal Rate of Return (IRR)
  • Payback Period
  • Resource Utilization Rate
  • Change Order Frequency
  • Accident Frequency Rate
  • Defect Density
  • Project Completion Rate
  • Staff Turnover Rate
